Lasagna

Description

A dish made of layers of noodles, meat sauce, and bechamel.

The best version I've ever had came from the restaurant Cafelatte in Honolulu, Hawaii.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb Italian sausage
  • 1 large yellow onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup dry red wine (like Merlot or Cabernet Sauvignon), optional
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (optional, for a little heat)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

For the Béchamel Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups whole milk, warmed
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg, freshly grated
  • Salt and white pepper to taste

For the Lasagna Assembly:

  • 1 lb lasagna noodles (regular or no-boil)
  • 1 (15 ounce) container whole milk ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, plus more for topping
  • 2 lbs whole milk mozzarella cheese, shredded

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce:
    1. In a large Dutch oven or heavy-bottomed pot, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef and Italian sausage and cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until browned. Drain off any excess fat.
    2. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5-7 minutes. Stir in the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
    3. If using, pour in the red wine and scrape up any browned bits from the bottom of the pot. Let the wine reduce by about half.
    4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste. Add the oregano, basil, red pepper flakes (if using), salt, and pepper.
    5. Bring the sauce to a simmer, then reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it cook for at least 1 hour, or up to 3 hours for a richer flavor. Stir occasionally.
  2. Prepare the Béchamel Sauce:
    1. While the meat sauce simmers, prepare the béchamel. In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
    2. Whisk in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes until it forms a smooth paste (this is a roux).
    3. Gradually whisk in the warm milk until the sauce is smooth. Bring to a gentle simmer, stirring constantly, until the sauce thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon, about 8-10 minutes.
    4. Remove from the heat and stir in the nutmeg, salt, and white pepper.
  3. Prepare the Lasagna Noodles:
    • If using regular noodles: Cook the lasagna noodles according to package directions until al dente. Drain and lay them flat on a lightly oiled baking sheet to prevent sticking.
    • If using no-boil noodles: You can proceed directly to assembly. They will cook in the sauce.
  4. Prepare the Ricotta Mixture: In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, beaten egg, and 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese. Mix until well combined.
  5. Assemble the Lasagna:
    1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    3. Arrange a single layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    4. Spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
    5. Top with a layer of meat sauce, followed by a generous layer of shredded mozzarella and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese.
    6. Spoon a thin layer of béchamel sauce over the mozzarella.
    7. Repeat the layers: noodles, the remaining ricotta mixture, meat sauce, mozzarella, Parmesan, and béchamel.
    8. For the final layer, place the remaining noodles on top, spread with the rest of the meat sauce and béchamel, and top generously with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake and Rest:
    1. Cover the baking dish with foil, making sure the foil doesn't touch the cheese (you can tent it slightly).
    2. Bake for 45 minutes.
    3. Remove the foil and bake for another 15-20 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and bubbly.
    4. Let the lasagna rest for at least 15-20 minutes before slicing and serving. This will help the layers set and make for cleaner slices.

Enjoy your delicious, homemade classic lasagna!
